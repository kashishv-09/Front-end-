Amidst the eclectic charm of hardware stores,
boutique hotels and cafes along Hamilton Road,
a new artisanal bakery cafe concept has entered the fray – La Levain.


La Levain means starter or leavening agent made from flour and water in French. The baking journey begins with it and the cafe will intrigue you through an
experience of the artisanal baking journey with a bevy of French classics and creative bakes.

With the cosy space outfitted in industrial-like grey interiors, rustic wooden furniture and neon orange lighting, along with a show kitchen to watch fresh bakes
being baked on the spot, this minimalist cafe lets you find your inner calm away from the hustle in a central yet off-beat Jalan Besar enclave. Perfect for cafe-
hoppers, dates, social gatherings and family friendly crowds with its provision of cool coloring sets as you enjoy the comforting brunch fare, delish baked treats
and specialty coffee.




Our Story


La Levain is not just an artisanal bakery cafe. Le Levain is a baking journey. It is an experience
created out of experiences amassed along the way.

With an emphasis on artisanal bakes created through Chef Wythe’s personal travel
encounters and yearning for new and innovative baking experiences,the selection of
pastries is designed to create an experiential journey, one of indulgence in French classics and
more baked treats. With you in mind.



Meet The Chef​


Founded by the well-heeled Executive Pastry Chef Wythe Soon who honed his baking skills in Japan, he was previously the head baker at Bakery Brera and Keong Saik Bakery.

He has also been named as a World Gourmet Awards Baker of the Year finalist.

Known for his artisanal bakes from insta-worthy cruffins, croissants to light and fluffy panettone made from Italian mother yeast, Chef Wythe is one of the baking maestros who likes to share his baking journey through his travel experiences, be it in search of the finest breads or a mastery of artisanal baking techniques.

Born into a family that runs a traditional Chinese bakery and pastry business in Malaysia, it is little wonder he has cake and pastry making in his blood coursing through his veins.

With this powerful synergy with a youthful and passionate Executive Chef – who loves to be involved in research & development and innovate by fusing premium ingredients in his much sought after artisanal bakes.
